About me:

My name is Gregory Delgado. I was born and raised in Houston, TX. I graduated from Atascocita High School in 2013. I enlisted in the Army in 2015 as an Air Defense Battle Management System Operator. I completed basic training in Fort Jackson, SC and attend advanced individual training in Fort Sill, OK. My duty stations are as follows: Camp Casey, Fort Sill, and Osan AB. I served in Afghanistan from 2018-2019 in support of Operation Freedom Sentinel. I completed my enlisted contract in November of 2021 and now reside in Houston, TX. I am currently a student at Devcode Camp and soon to be a web developer. I am engaged and have a beautiful one year old daughter named, Lucille. Some hobbies of mine include: going to the gym, painting, watching movies, 3D modeling, and playing video games.
